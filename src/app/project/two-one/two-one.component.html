Product Recommendation is a web application that recommends products to users based on their purchase history. The application is designed to help users discover new products that may be intrested in.

Users can view product details such as, product name, description, price and availability and add products to cart directly. When a user purchase a product, the system analyzes their purchase history and recomends five other products that are related to their purchase.These recommendations are based on various factor, such as user's previous purchase, products that are bought together and popularity od products in user's area.

Overall, Product Recommendation is a powerful web application that provides personalized recommendations to users. With its user-friendly interface and efficient recommendation algorithm, application helps users discover new products and improve their overall shopping experience.
